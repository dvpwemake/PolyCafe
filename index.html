About Us

Polypinion Cafe, a haven for independent thinkers. At Polypinion, we embody the ethos of individual thought, offering a unique experience encapsulated in our slogan: "Taste of Opinions." We exclusively source ethically harvested and traded coffee, respecting the principles of sustainability and fostering a harmonious coexistence between humanity and nature.

What sets us apart is our collaboration with champions in the coffee world. Proudly working with the 2022 World Barista Champion and the 2023 Australian Barista Champion, we ensure an unparalleled standard of excellence and consistency in every cup. Polypinion Cafe is not just a coffee destination; it's a celebration of independent minds, ethical choices, and the delightful harmony found in a finely brewed cup. Join us for a coffee experience that goes beyond the ordinary.

Polyaragon

Guatemala

$57.99

(1 kg)

This Antigua Aragon comes from Alejandro Andrade's farm and the Bella Vista processing mill, owned and operated by fourth-generation coffee farmer Luis Pedro Zelaya and his family. The Antigua coffee region is one of the better known in Guatemala, not only for its quality, but also for the beautiful colonial city that attracts visitors from all over the world.

Surrounded by 3 volcanos, it sits over 1,500 meters above sea level and has ideal coffee-growing conditions: rich soils, ample rain and sun, and consistent temperatures. This washed caturra lot is grown at an altitude of 1800 masl and exhibits a clean, sweet cup profile with notes of orange, caramel and white peach.
